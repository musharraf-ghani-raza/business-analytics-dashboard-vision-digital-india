## What this project does

A full analytics solution built for a real company — not a practice dataset.
It monitors live KPIs, tracks SLA performance week-by-week, logs client queries,
and delivers automated MIS reports to internal stakeholders — reducing manual
reporting effort by ~30%.

## Why I built this

During my internship at Vision Digital India (Jan–Mar 2025), the team was
tracking performance across 6 service categories using scattered spreadsheets.
There was no single view of SLA health, CSAT trends, or revenue by service.
I built this dashboard to fix that — and it's now used for weekly reporting.

## Dashboards

### Excel (5 sheets)
- **Raw data** — 72 rows across 6 service categories × 12 months
- **MIS Monthly Report** — 6 KPI cards, SLA status, service-wise breakdown
- **SLA Dashboard** — 12-week compliance tracker with trend indicators
- **Client Query Log** — 22 queries with priority, resolution time, and status
- **Pivot Summary** — Revenue pivot table + clustered bar chart

### Power BI (7 visuals)
- KPI cards, weekly SLA trend, ticket status donut, revenue by service,
  CSAT by service, monthly volume stacked bar, SLA performance table
