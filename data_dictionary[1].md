## Column Definitions

|---|---|---|---|
| `Month` | Text | Calendar month and year | Jan 2025 |

| `Tickets_Raised` | Integer | Total support tickets raised in the month | 145 |
| `Tickets_Resolved` | Integer | Total tickets resolved in the month | 138 |
| `SLA_Breaches` | Integer | Number of tickets that breached SLA threshold | 5 |
| `Avg_Resolution_hrs` | Decimal | Average ticket resolution time in hours | 4.8 |
| `CSAT_Score_pct` | Decimal | Customer satisfaction score as a percentage | 89.2 |

## KPI Definitions

| KPI | Formula | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Resolution Rate | (Tickets Resolved / Tickets Raised) × 100 | ≥ 90% |
| SLA Compliance Rate | ((Tickets Raised - SLA Breaches) / Tickets Raised) × 100 | ≥ 92% |
| CSAT Score | Average of CSAT_Score_pct across services | ≥ 85% |
| Avg Resolution Time | Average of Avg_Resolution_hrs | ≤ 6 hours |
